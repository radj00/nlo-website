Welcome to Nograles Law Offices

Established in 1991, Nograles Law has since then successfully guided its clients in the field of Labor and Labor Relations. Nograles Law has provided legal strategies and solutions to its clients' labor issues and concerns, including illegal dismissal cases, corporate reorganizations and redundancy programs; corporate mergers and acquisitions affecting employee rights; strikes; CBA negotiations; and the hottest topics of the season - "endo", contractualization, and joint assessment cases. Indeed, Nograles Law's three decades of experience in the specialized field of Labor has enabled the Firm to quickly and effectively resolve a wide spectrum of Labor matters.

THE FIRM

We have spent thirty-three years in the field of labor and employer practices. We have guided and assisted our clients through it all - mundane or complex: illegal dismissal cases, sexual harassment, corporate reorganization, redundancy programs, employer obligations vis-à-vis corporate restructuring, mergers and acquisitions, and the hottest topic of the decade - endo, outsourcing, contractualization, and joint assessments by the DOLE.

Nograles Law associates have true grit and integrity that, together with the partners of our firm, form a legal team that is truly accountable for our clients' success.

Atty. Soledad F. Nograles

Senior Partner

Atty. Soledad F. Nograles is a Senior Partner of the Firm. She is a certified Chemical Engineer, a 1981 Ateneo Law graduate, a bar topnotcher of the 1982 Bar Exams, and a Bar Examiner in Labor in 2013.

Atty. Clarence Rommel C. Nanquil

Senior Partner

Clarence Rommel C. Nanquil is a 2005 Ateneo Law graduate, and the former Executive Editor and Lead Editor of the Ateneo de Manila Law Journal. He has successfully handled numerous complicated labor issues at all levels, from the Labor Arbiter, all the way to the Supreme Court, as well as Labor Relations matters, including CBA negotiations.

Atty. Joannes Vinarao-Pilapil

Senior Partner

Joannes Vinarao-Pilapil is a partner of the Firm who specializes in dispute resolution, particularly negotiation, mediation and commercial arbitration. She is an LLB degree holder from the University of the Philippines College of Law and then obtained her Master of Laws (LLM) degree in Alternative Dispute Resolution from the Cardozo School of Law in New York, where she was granted a Dean’s Merit Scholarship, and later conferred a Cardozo Service and Achievement Award. She is a mediator of the Office of Alternative Dispute Resolution (OADR), Department of Justice and the National Center for Mediation (NCM). She had worked for eight (8) years as a Court Attorney at the Supreme Court and Court of Appeals, which accounts for her extensive juristic exposure and expertise.

Atty. Amanda Marie F. Nograles

Senior Partner

Amanda Marie F. Nograles is a partner (on leave) of the firm who specializes in corporate law. She graduated from the Ateneo Law School, with second honors, in 2011. She worked at Romulo Mabanta Buenaventura Sayoc and de los Angeles for six years where she honed her practice in securities and corporate law.
