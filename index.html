WELCOME TO NOGRALES LAW OFFICES

Nograles Law Offices was established in 1991. For over thirty-three (33) years, the Firm has been engaged in Labor Law practice. It provides counsel and legal advice to clients, which include players in major industries. If and when issues unavoidably reach the labor courts, the Firm is prepared to represent its clients in their cases. Owing to the Firm’s long experience in Labor Litigation, it is able to defend its clients’ cause, relying on a combination of tested legal strategy and applicable jurisprudence. With this advantage, the Firm’s success rate in the handling of cases has always been its source of pride and inspiration.

The Firm’s Founding Partner is George C. Nograles, a 1979 Ateneo Law School graduate, who has practiced in the field of Labor and Labor Relations since 1980.

Soledad F. Nograles is a Senior Partner of the Firm. She is a certified Chemical Engineer, a 1981 Ateneo Law graduate, a bar topnotcher of the 1982 Bar Exams, and a Bar Examiner in Labor in 2013.

Clarence Rommel C. Nanquil, and Joannes Vinarao Pilapil are also Senior Partners of the Firm.

Clarence Nanquil is a 2005 Ateneo Law graduate, and the former Executive Editor and Lead Editor of the Ateneo de Manila Law Journal. He has successfully handled numerous complicated labor issues at all levels, from the Labor Arbiter, all the way to the Supreme Court, as well as Labor Relations matters, including CBA negotiations.

Upon the other hand, Joannes Pilapil, a UP Law graduate, has a Master’s Degree (LLM) in Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) at the Cardozo School of Law in New York, USA. She is an accredited mediator of the Department of Justice, Office of Alternative Dispute Resolution, and National Center for Mediation (NCM). Currently, she is the Vice-President for Advocacy in Public Education of the Philippine Institute of Arbitrators. Aside from Labor Litigation, Atty Pilapil handles commercial arbitration cases for the Firm.

With our above roster of leaders, and with the able assistance of Associates who are graduates of leading law schools, we hope to be able continue to guide and assist clients in their Labor concerns.
